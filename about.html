About Us

Us

ICICI Bank is a leading private sector bank in India. The Bank's consolidated total assets stood at Rs. 14.76 trillion at September 30, 2020. ICICI Bank currently has a network of 5,288 branches and 15,158 ATMs across India.

Our First CEO

Kamath appointed the Managing Director and CEO of ICICI Ltd. ICICI Ltd was the first intermediary to move away from single prime rate to three-tier prime rates structure and introduced yield-curve based pricing. 1998: The name The Industrial Credit and Investment Corporation of India Ltd changed to ICICI Ltd.
